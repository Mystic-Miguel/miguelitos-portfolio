About Me

I am a software developer with over 5 years of experience in web and mobile application development. I specialize in front-end development, particularly in React and Vue.js, and have a strong background in back-end technologies like Node.js and Python. My passion for technology drives me to constantly learn and adapt to new challenges.

I began my career at Tech Solutions Inc., where I developed several high-impact web applications. Later, I joined Innovatech as a senior developer, leading a team to create scalable and user-friendly mobile apps. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Technology.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, reading about the latest tech trends, and contributing to open-source projects. I'm also an avid traveler, always eager to explore new cultures and cuisines.
